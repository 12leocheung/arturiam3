🎹 MiniLab 3 Guidebook

⚡ SUPER SIMPLE (30 seconds)

Three steps, that's it:

1️⃣ Press REC pad

2️⃣ Play notes on keyboard

3️⃣ Press REC again to stop

✓ Your clip is recorded & looping! Press REC again for the next clip, or click pads to play them together.

🎯 Quick Record (5 seconds!)

1. Press REC pad (red, Pad 7) — see "● REC ARMED"

2. Click any pad 1-8 (or press keys) → recording starts

3. Play notes on keyboard

4. Press REC again to stop & save

The pad you click becomes your recording target. You can record up to 8 different clips this way, then play them back together by clicking them in Clip Mode (Pad 3).

👀 What You'll See

When you press REC: Red pad pulses, OLED shows "REC CLIP 1 - Play now!"

While recording: The pad shows "●" (recording icon), OLED counts beats

After you stop: Clip starts looping automatically, pad shows "▶" (play icon)

Ready to record more: Press REC again to record to Clip 2, etc.

Space → Play/Stop transport

1-8 → Record clips (when REC armed)

Tab → Toggle Piano/Drum mode

Z-M, A-G → Play notes (Piano)

Q-P → Black keys

🎚️ Knobs (Top & Bottom Rows)

Top: Cutoff, Resonance, LFO Rate, LFO Depth

Bottom: Attack, Decay, Sustain, Release

Drag up/down or scroll wheel to adjust

🎚️ Faders (4 Columns)

1: Master Volume

2: Filter Envelope Amount

3: LFO Target (0-1)

4: Effects Mix

🎚️ Touch Strips (Left Side)

Pitch: Bend pitch up/down, auto-returns to center

Mod: Add modulation (filter/tone effects)

🔄 Encoder (Middle Top)

Scroll/drag to cycle through knob parameters

Shows current knob value in OLED display

Click to select

🎛️ Pads (8 Control Buttons) - Detailed

🔶 Pad 1 (Blue) - ARP:

Toggles Arpeggiator mode. When armed, playing a note will automatically arpeggiate up/down the scale. Useful for creating flowing melodic patterns without manual playing.

▦ Pad 2 (Blue) - DRUM MODE:

Switch between Piano and Drum modes. In Drum Mode, the keys trigger drum samples (kick, snare, hi-hat, etc.). The 8 pads themselves play different drum sounds. Tap or click to trigger samples.

🔄 Pad 3 (Orange) - LOOP/CLIP MODE:

Enters Clip Launch mode where all 8 pads become clip launchers. Each pad launches/stops a different audio clip. Click pads to play, stop, or queue clips. Essential for building multi-track arrangements.

↻ Pad 4 (Blue) - PROGRAM CHANGE:

Sends MIDI program change commands (when used with external synths). Allows quick switching between different synth presets/programs without manual MIDI configuration.

⏹ Pad 5 (Blue) - STOP:

Stops playback immediately. Halts all active notes and clips, returns transport to stopped state. Same as pressing Space bar. All playing clips and notes are silenced.

▶ Pad 6 (Green) - PLAY/START:

Starts playback from the beginning. Transport begins running, clips start at beat 1, and the internal clock begins syncing. Use this or Space bar to start the session.

● Pad 7 (Red) - REC (RECORD ARM):

This is your main recording button! Press to arm recording mode (pulsing red indicator appears). Then press any pad 1-8 (or keys 1-8) to start recording to that clip. Transport auto-starts. Press again to stop recording. Everything you play is captured into that clip and can be looped/arranged.

✦ Pad 8 (Yellow) - TAP TEMPO:

Set BPM by tapping in rhythm. Click this pad multiple times at your desired tempo (minimum 2 taps). The BPM updates based on the timing between taps. Visual feedback shows in OLED. Great for syncing to external beats or your own timing preference.

⬅️ Left Control Buttons

Shift: Toggle shift mode

Oct+/-: Change octave ±3

Up/Down: Navigate parameters

🎹 Piano Keyboard (25 Keys)

White keys play notes C3-C5

Black keys play sharps/flats

Use Oct+/- buttons to shift range

🎵 Transport & BPM

Play: Pad 6 or Space

Stop: Pad 5 or Space

BPM: Double-click BPM number or scroll on it (40-220)

Tap Tempo: Click Pad 8 in rhythm to set BPM

💾 Clip Launcher

Press Pad 3 (Loop) to enter Clip Mode

Click any pad to play/stop clips

Clips loop at 1 bar (adjustable)

🎼 Typical Workflow

Step 1 - Set Tempo: Click Pad 8 (Tap Tempo) 4+ times at your desired tempo, or double-click the BPM number to enter manually.

Step 2 - Arm Recording: Press Pad 7 (REC) — watch for pulsing red indicator.

Step 3 - Record Clip: Press Pad 1 or key 1 to start recording to first clip. Transport auto-starts.

Step 4 - Play & Record: Use keyboard to play notes (Z-M, A-G for white keys). Your performance is automatically recorded into the clip.

Step 5 - Stop Recording: Press Pad 7 again. Clip saves and loops.

Step 6 - Add More Clips: Repeat from Step 3 with different pads (2, 3, 4, etc.) to layer multiple clips.

Step 7 - Playback & Arrangement: Use Pad 6 (Play) and Pad 5 (Stop) to control the transport. Click different pads to trigger/stop individual clips on the fly.
